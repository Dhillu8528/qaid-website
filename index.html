Quest for Alternative Interventions in Development (QAID)

Empowering Communities Through Education, Employment, Health, and Financial Inclusion

Welcome to QAID

QAID is an organization committed to driving sustainable development initiatives, specifically amongst economically marginalized Muslim communities. Our core focus areas are education, employment, health, skill development, and financial inclusion.

Our Projects

  • Education Outreach: Bridging learning gaps through community-led schooling.
  • Skill Development: Vocational training for youth and women.
  • Health Camps: Promoting preventive healthcare access.
  • Microfinance Assistance: Supporting entrepreneurship with financial literacy.

About Us

Founded by MD Muhammad Dhanish, QAID is a grassroots movement built on the belief that inclusive development is the cornerstone of a better future. Our mission is to catalyze social equity through targeted interventions tailored to the real needs of the community.
